🐾 How to Use

  1. 🧭This is for Telengana only
  2. 🔍 Search your area or pincode using the search box
  3. 🌐 Discover the feeder name in the resultbox and zoom into the marker for location
  4. 📍 To add yourself,/b> click on the map to select your exact location
  5. 👤 Fill in your name and phone number
  6. ✅ Submit to register as a feeder in your area
  7. 📞 Request contact of existing feeders by clicking on their marker

All submissions are reviewed before appearing on the map.
